# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Houston drivers, nearby residents, dealership staff, rental operators, and small business fleet managers who need a fast, clear way to call, get directions, compare detailing services, or request an appointment.

## Product Purpose

Clean Max II Auto Detail is a single-page local service website for a Houston car detailing shop. It should turn the physical storefront identity into a useful digital experience with clear services, visible pricing, appointment requests, location details, and persistent call and directions actions.

## Brand Personality

Bright, direct, local, service-focused, and confident. The page should feel like a cleaned-up digital version of the shop's blue wall, yellow signage, royal-blue lettering, and "Experience & Quality" promise.

## Anti-references

Do not make a generic black, white, and gray luxury detailing site. Do not use dark automotive showroom styling, vague corporate gradients, tiny menu-board text, generic car logos, or overly polished templates that erase the neighborhood Houston shop identity.

## Design Principles

1. Preserve the storefront signal: sky-blue wall texture, yellow panels, royal-blue type, and high-visibility service-board rhythm are the brand.
2. Make action obvious: call, directions, and request a detail should always be easy to reach.
3. Keep pricing readable: pricing is real HTML with dotted leaders and mobile-friendly cards, never a shrunken menu image.
4. Feel local and trustworthy: use direct copy, clear contact details, and service proof instead of luxury cliches.
5. Build for phones first: large tap targets, sticky actions, readable forms, and collapsible service menus.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, labeled form fields, reduced-motion support, descriptive alt text for all images, and large touch targets for mobile users.
